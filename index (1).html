✦ Our Story

Crafting Love Stories
One Wedding at a Time

At Weddings by Fourth Munky, we believe every love story deserves a breathtakingly beautiful celebration. Based in the heart of Dehradun, we bring your dream wedding to life with meticulous attention to detail, creative vision, and seamless execution.

From intimate gatherings amid the foothills of the Himalayas to grand destination affairs at luxury resorts, we curate every element — flowers, décor, lighting, venue, catering and more — to reflect the unique essence of your bond.

With deep roots in Uttarakhand and connections across India's finest venues, our passionate team works tirelessly behind the scenes so you can live entirely in the moment on your most precious day.
